## Strength Sessions

### Session A — Upper / Push-Pull (~60 min)

**Warm-up (5 min)**
- Arm circles, band pull-aparts or shoulder CARs, 10 scapular push-ups, hip flexor stretch

**Pull-ups / Calf raises superset**
- 4 rounds: pull-ups × 8 → immediately calf raises × 15, rest 90 sec
- Calf raises: slow 2-sec up, pause at top, 2-sec down — bodyweight to start; add load (dumbbell in one hand) if these feel trivial
- If you drop a pull-up rep on set 4, note it and move on

**Dips** (optional — try it)
- 3 × 8–10 bodyweight dips, rest 60 sec
- If dips aren't available or feel sketchy: fall back to the push-up superset (3 rounds: 10 standard + 10 incline on rack bar)

**KB row (single arm)**
- 3 × 10 @ 44 lbs — RPE was 9.0 last block; hold here. Drop to 35 if form breaks.

**Barbell overhead press (standing)**
- 3 × 10 @ 50 lbs — moving from DBs to barbell; same load, +2 reps. Add weight when 3×10 feels solid.

**Core finisher (8 min)**
- 3 rounds:
  - 30 sec dead bug (alternate arm/leg, lower back glued down)
  - 30 sec hollow hold
  - 10 push-up to downward dog

### Session B — Lower / Posterior Chain (~60 min)

**Warm-up (5 min)**
- Hip circles, leg swings front/side, 10 glute bridges, 10 banded clamshells

**Squat**
- 4 × 10 @ 95 / 115 / 135 / 150 lbs — try 150 on the last set (was 145 @ RPE 7.5)
- If 150 doesn't feel clean, hold at 145

**Romanian Deadlift**
- 4 × 10 @ 50 / 70 / 85 / 100 lbs — nudging up from 45/65/85/95

**KB Swing**
- 3 × 15 @ 35 lbs — bumping reps from 12 (RPE was 7.0, too easy). Hip drive, not a squat.

**Bulgarian split squats / Calf raises superset** (new — expect to drop weight on BSS)
- 3 rounds: split squats × 8 each leg @ 35–40 lbs → immediately calf raises × 15, rest 90 sec
- BSS: rear foot elevated on bench or chair; find your footing first set
- Calf raises: 2-sec up, pause at top, 2-sec down — bodyweight or add load if trivial

**Core finisher (8 min)**
- 3 rounds:
  - 90 sec plank (posterior tilt — tuck pelvis, don't let hips sag)
  - 10 hanging knee raises (on pull-up bar — progress to straight-leg when these feel easy)
