## Strength Sessions

### Session A — Upper / Push-Pull (~60 min)

**Warm-up (5 min)**
- Arm circles, band pull-aparts or shoulder CARs, 10 scapular push-ups

**Pull-up progression**
- 4 x 6 pull-ups — full hang to chin over bar, controlled lowering (3 sec down)
- Rest 90 sec between sets. If rep 6 feels easy, add a 5-second dead hang after each set.

**Push-up superset** (pair A + B, rest 60 sec between rounds, 3 rounds)
- A: 10 standard push-ups — hands shoulder-width, elbows 45 degrees
- B: 10 incline push-ups on rack bar — emphasises upper chest and shoulder stability

**Dumbbell row**
- 3 x 10 each side — elbow drives back, pause at top, slow return
- Use a weight where rep 10 is genuinely hard

**KB or DB overhead press**
- 3 x 8 — standing, single arm or double. Brace core, ribs down, don't arch lower back.

**Core finisher (8 min)**
- 3 rounds:
  - 30 sec dead bug (alternate arm/leg, lower back glued to floor)
  - 30 sec hollow hold
  - 10 push-up to downward dog (flows into hip flexor stretch)

---

### Session B — Lower / Posterior Chain (~60 min)

**Warm-up (5 min)**
- Hip circles, leg swings front/side, 10 glute bridges, 10 banded clamshells if available

**Squat**
- 4 x 6 — goblet squat with heavy KB or barbell back squat, your call
- Cue: chest up, drive knees out over toes, full depth. Hamstrings should feel loaded at the bottom.

**Romanian Deadlift (RDL)**
- 3 x 8 — DB or barbell, slow hinge, feel the hamstring stretch at the bottom
- This is the single most important movement for your hamstring health. Own the hinge.

**KB Swing**
- 3 x 15 — hip drive, not a squat. Glutes fire at the top, hamstrings load on the way back.

**Single-leg work** (pair A + B, 3 rounds)
- A: 8 reverse lunges each leg — step back, knee hovers, drive through front heel to stand
- B: 8 single-leg glute bridges each side — slow, squeeze at top, keep hips level

**Core finisher (8 min)**
- 3 rounds:
  - 40 sec plank — posterior tilt (tuck pelvis slightly, don't let hips sag)
  - 10 ab wheel rollouts or 10 cable/band Pallof press each side
  - 10 KB deadlifts (light, perfect hinge pattern — treat it as a cool-down)
