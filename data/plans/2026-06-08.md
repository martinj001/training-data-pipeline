## Strength Sessions

### Session A — Upper / Push-Pull (~60 min)

**Warm-up (5 min)**
- Arm circles, band pull-aparts or shoulder CARs, 10 scapular push-ups, hip flexor stretch

**Pull-up progression**
- Week 1: 4 × 6 — rest 90 sec, controlled 3-sec lowering
- Week 2: try 4 × 7. If you drop a rep on set 4, that's fine — you're at the edge of the load.

**Push-up superset** (pair A + B, rest 60 sec, 3 rounds)
- A: 10 standard push-ups
- B: 10 incline push-ups on rack bar

**Dumbbell row**
- 3 × 10 — try 30 lbs this block (was 25 lbs all of block 1). Drop back to 25 if form breaks down.

**KB or DB overhead press**
- 3 × 8 — try 45 lbs (was 40 lbs). If form breaks on rep 6, stay at 40. No arching the lower back.

**Core finisher (8 min)**
- 3 rounds:
  - 30 sec dead bug (alternate arm/leg, lower back glued down)
  - 30 sec hollow hold
  - 10 push-up to downward dog

---

### Session B — Lower / Posterior Chain (~60 min)

**Warm-up (5 min)**
- Hip circles, leg swings front/side, 10 glute bridges, 10 banded clamshells

**Squat**
- 4 × 10 @ 95 / 115 / 115 / 135 lbs
- Week 2: try 145 on the last set if the 135 set felt comfortable last time

**Romanian Deadlift**
- Week 1: 4 × 8 @ 45 / 65 / 75 / 88 lbs (hold block 1 final weights)
- Week 2: 4 × 8 @ 50 / 70 / 80 / 95 lbs — nudge everything ~5–7 lbs
- This is still the most important movement for your hamstrings. Own the hinge.

**KB Swing**
- 3 × 10 @ 26 lbs — these got skipped in block 1. Do these.
- Hip drive, not a squat. Hamstrings load on the way back, glutes fire at the top.

**Reverse lunges**
- Week 1: 3 × 10 each leg @ 40 lbs
- Week 2: try 45 lbs

**Core finisher (8 min)**
- 3 rounds:
  - 90 sec plank (posterior tilt — tuck pelvis, don't let hips sag)
  - 10 ab wheel rollouts
