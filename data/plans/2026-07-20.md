## Strength Sessions

### Session A — Upper / Push-Pull (~60 min)

**Warm-up (5 min)**
- Arm circles, band pull-aparts or shoulder CARs, 10 scapular push-ups, hip flexor stretch

**Pull-ups / Calf raises superset**
- 4 rounds: pull-ups × 8 → immediately calf raises × 15, rest 90 sec
- Jul 21: held 8,8,8,8 (no dropped rep this time) @ RPE 7/8/9/10. Hold at 8 — it's fine if RPE climbs by set 4.
- **Standing Barbell Calf Raise** (bar racked across upper back like a back squat), 2-sec up, pause at top, 2-sec down — **bodyweight only (0 lbs), not a progression lift.** Reframed Jul 21: this is calf health/stretch maintenance for running/cycling, not a strength exercise to load up. Stays at bodyweight indefinitely.

**Push-up superset**
- 3 rounds: 15 standard push-ups + 12 incline push-ups, rest 60 sec
- Jul 21 RPE jumped to 8–9 (was 7 last block) after ~2 weeks away from Session A — expected detraining effect on bodyweight endurance work, not a red flag.

**KB row (single arm)**
- 3 × 10 @ 35 lbs — working weight, hold.

**Barbell overhead press (standing)**
- 3 × 8 @ 50 lbs — holding here, not the 55 bump. RPE steady at 8–9, held up fine through the layoff (unlike the bodyweight work above).

**Core finisher (8 min)**
- 3 rounds of hanging knee raises + hollow hold, but only 1 round of push-up to downward dog — the other 2 rounds are now plank instead (Jul 21 call):
  - 10 hanging knee raises (progress to straight-leg when these feel easy)
  - 20 sec hollow hold — RPE was 9.0 two blocks ago, 8.0 Jul 21, hold here
  - Round 1: 10 push-up to downward dog. Rounds 2–3: 90 sec plank (posterior tilt — tuck pelvis, don't let hips sag)

## Trainer tempo — rotating options

Default now leans toward the structured options (Sweet Spot / Over-Unders) rather than easy Z3 — pick whichever fits the day, but favor 1 or 2 unless recovery says otherwise. If riding outdoors instead (or FTS MTB is on), no structure required.

**Option 1 — Sweet Spot**
- 10 min easy warm-up (Z2)
- 2 × 15 min sweet spot (top of Z3 / bottom of Z4), 5 min Z2 recovery between
- 10 min cooldown

**Option 2 — Over-Unders**
- 10 min easy warm-up (Z2)
- 3 × 10 min alternating 2 min over-threshold (Z4) / 2 min under (Z3), 5 min Z2 recovery between sets
- 10 min cooldown

**Option 3 — Classic Z3 tempo (fallback / lower-key day)**
- 10 min easy warm-up (Z2)
- 4 × 8 min Z3 ("comfortably hard" — can talk, wouldn't want to), 5 min Z2 recovery between
- 5 min cooldown
