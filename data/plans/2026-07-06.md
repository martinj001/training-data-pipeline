## Strength Sessions

### Session A — Upper / Push-Pull (~60 min)

**Warm-up (5 min)**
- Arm circles, band pull-aparts or shoulder CARs, 10 scapular push-ups, hip flexor stretch

**Pull-ups / Calf raises superset**
- 4 rounds: pull-ups × 8 → immediately calf raises × 15, rest 90 sec
- Last block held 4×7 at RPE 7–10; this is the deferred attempt at 8. Dropping a rep on set 4 is fine.
- **Standing Barbell Calf Raise** (bar racked across upper back like a back squat), 2-sec up, pause at top, 2-sec down — switching off the single-DB version since 25 lbs (your heaviest DB) has had this sitting at RPE ~5 for two sessions with no room left to add. Start at 65 lbs (bar + light plates), add 10 lbs once RPE drops back under 6.

**Push-up superset** (replacing the dips slot — gym reconfig hasn't happened in two straight blocks; bumping reps since bodyweight volume has room to grow. You're testing the dip setup this week — if it's ready, sub dips back in for a session and log it so we can compare)
- 3 rounds: 15 standard push-ups + 12 incline push-ups, rest 60 sec

**KB row (single arm)**
- 3 × 10 @ 35 lbs — this is your working weight now, not a fallback. Two sessions of "35 = better form" says it's the right load.

**Barbell overhead press (standing)**
- 3 × 8 @ 55 lbs — bumping from 3×10 @ 50 (RPE 8–9, ready for more). Stay at 55 if form breaks before rep 6.

**Core finisher (8 min)**
- 3 rounds:
  - 10 hanging knee raises (progress to straight-leg when these feel easy)
  - 20 sec hollow hold — trimming from 30 sec; it's been sitting at RPE 10 for three sessions in a row with no room to progress, so cutting time instead of grinding at max effort
  - 10 push-up to downward dog

## Trainer tempo — rotating options

Pick a different one each time you're on the trainer (Wed, or Sat if it becomes a ride instead of a run) so it's not the same workout twice in a row. If riding outdoors instead, no structure required.

**Option 1 — Sweet Spot**
- 10 min easy warm-up (Z2)
- 2 × 15 min sweet spot (top of Z3 / bottom of Z4), 5 min Z2 recovery between
- 10 min cooldown

**Option 2 — Over-Unders**
- 10 min easy warm-up (Z2)
- 3 × 10 min alternating 2 min over-threshold (Z4) / 2 min under (Z3), 5 min Z2 recovery between sets
- 10 min cooldown

**Option 3 — Classic Z3 tempo**
- 10 min easy warm-up (Z2)
- 4 × 8 min Z3 ("comfortably hard" — can talk, wouldn't want to), 5 min Z2 recovery between
- 5 min cooldown
