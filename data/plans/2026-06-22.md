## Strength Sessions

### Session A — Upper / Push-Pull (~60 min)

**Warm-up (5 min)**
- Arm circles, band pull-aparts or shoulder CARs, 10 scapular push-ups, hip flexor stretch

**Pull-up progression**
- Week 1: 4 × 7 — rest 90 sec, controlled 3-sec lowering
- Week 2: try 4 × 8. If you drop a rep on set 4, that's fine — note it and move on.

**Push-up superset** (pair A + B, rest 60 sec, 3 rounds)
- A: 10 standard push-ups
- B: 10 incline push-ups on rack bar

**Dumbbell row**
- 3 × 10 @ 35 lbs — stepping up from 30 lbs. Drop to 30 if form breaks down.

**KB or DB overhead press**
- Week 1: 3 × 8 @ 50 lbs
- Week 2: try 55 lbs. If form breaks on rep 6, stay at 50.

**Core finisher (8 min)**
- 3 rounds:
  - 30 sec dead bug (alternate arm/leg, lower back glued down)
  - 30 sec hollow hold
  - 10 push-up to downward dog

---

### Session B — Lower / Posterior Chain (~60 min)

**Warm-up (5 min)**
- Hip circles, leg swings front/side, 10 glute bridges, 10 banded clamshells

**Squat**
- Week 1: 4 × 10 @ 95 / 115 / 135 / 145 lbs — attempt the 145 that got deferred in block 2
- Week 2: hold 145 on last set or try 150 if week 1 felt clean

**Romanian Deadlift**
- Week 1: 4 × 10 @ 50 / 70 / 80 / 95 lbs (hold block 2 week 2 weights)
- Week 2: 4 × 10 @ 55 / 75 / 85 / 100 lbs

**KB Swing**
- 3 × 12 @ 26 lbs — bumping reps from 10 (RPE was 4–5, too easy). Hip drive, not a squat.

**Reverse lunges**
- Week 1: 3 × 10 each leg @ 45 lbs
- Week 2: try 50 lbs

**Core finisher (8 min)**
- 3 rounds:
  - 90 sec plank (posterior tilt — tuck pelvis, don't let hips sag)
  - 10 ab wheel rollouts
